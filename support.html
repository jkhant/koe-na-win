Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app?

Koe Na Win is a prayer tracking app designed to help you maintain your daily prayer routine. Simply log your daily prayers and track your progress.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not collect personal information. Your prayer progress is saved locally on your device and is completely private.

How can I reset my progress?

To reset progress, go to the Calendar tab and choose the "+" button. Then select a new date to start tracking from that point.
